Our Story

Uni5 was created by A Group of Filipino Young Entrepreneurs established in the year 2014 in Quezon City, Philippines that engaged in the Direct Selling of Royale Food Supplements, powdered beverages, Beauty, Skin, & personal care products through a unique distribution method providing income opportunity to all people. With our idea behind this Fast-growing organization worldwide build our mission and vision in the society.

Responding to the needs of region and country, Uni5 accepts the challenged to become the leading and top distributor of products of Royale Business Club International. With the dedication of Management Team, Uni5 is committed for a high quality and services to the customers.

Uni5 as the business model of delivering high-quality products wants to expand the operation all over the world. We are committed to change the lives of our Oversees Filipino Workers in Taiwan, Russian, India, Macau, Singapore, USA, and more. This requires us more than the patience, hard work, perseverance in all of our activities. A Management is continuously upgrading the programs that suit to the high quality of services to our customer.

About the Founder

"Hello and welcome to the UNI5 WELLNESS INTERNATIONAL website. As Founder, I am honored to welcome you to the website and to UNI5. The numbers of professionals located in more than 5 Provinces in the Philippines and 5 International Group have joined UNI5 to provide high quality service to all Filipinos worldwide."

"Uni5, on its humble beginning in the 3rd Quarter of 2014, assures to provide different programs that will benefit our Grand Leaders, Team Leaders, Business Partners, and all People in the society. Uni5 Metro Manila was launched in September2014; Uni5 Legazpi, Pampanga and Cavite was launched in October 2014, and more to open before the year end and beyond."

"Uni5 is becoming a mature organization and, like any organization reaching that milestone, has challenges and issues it must resolve. I come to this office wanting to ensure the association is available to future generations of Uni5 professionals. I look to the membership to help ensure its long term success through two key initiatives: integration of the strategic plan into the short term and long term planning of the various committees and teams that make up Uni5; and ensuring the financial health of the organization. Both of these are significant issues and I will be asking for your creative ideas to help us make the strategic plan a reality and ensure the financial health of our organization."

"Uni5 is an organization of its members and can progress only by the drive of the membership. I ask that you take an active role this year; whether it is as a committee member or taking on a leadership position as partner, grand leader, or agreeing to run later this year for an deserving position; your organization depends on you."

"I look forward to working with all of you over this year as we work to achieve our personal and professional goals. I would ask that you include Uni5 as part of your plan to achieve those goals, as an active member and part of Uni5 Family."
